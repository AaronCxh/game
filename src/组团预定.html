Corporate Events

There’s nothing like a truly engaging, immersive and unique experience to bring a team together. Get your team out of their day-to-day environment and challenge them with an event they’ll talk about all year! Perfect for team building, leadership development, corporate entertainment and EOFY and Christmas parties.

 

Our venue managers will take the stress out of organising an event and assist you through the process to make sure your session is a complete success. We cater for 6 to 90 people and tailor the in-game experience to suit your needs and budget.

 

Corporate Events are available Monday to Friday during normal business hours. To enquire about a booking during normal business hours, please submit the form below. For bookings outside these hours, please visit our Bookings Page.

School/University Groups

If you have students ready to try out the most engaging, immersive VR experiences on the planet then we can help you. Combine your students’ skills and interests for this unique group experience. We can hold school and uni bookings during the day at a time that best suits you and help cater an in-game length that will work best for your group. Our experiences are for ages 13 plus.

 

School/University Groups are available Monday to Friday during normal business hours. To enquire about a booking during normal business hours, please submit the form below. For bookings outside these hours, please visit our Bookings Page.

For all bookings outside normal business hours, please visit our Bookings and Frequently Asked Questions pages.
