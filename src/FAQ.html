Questions

TICKETS

Tickets are $88 $68 (20 dollars off during the trial run)per person for a 60-minute experience.

WHERE ARE THE VENUES LOCATED?

Sydney: Unit 9, 26 Dunning Avenue, Rosebery, NSW

OPERATING HOURS

Monday - Friday: 2pm - 10pm

Saturday - Sunday: 11am - 10pm

HOW MANY PEOPLE CAN PLAY AT ONCE?

A maximum of 8 players.

The ideal numbers of players for each session is between 4-8, if you have less than 4 players we suggest you can find a session which is already booked by other players and join them.

CONTACT NUMBER

(02) 9313 6135

CAN I WEAR MY GLASSES?

Glasses cannot be worn in this experience. We recommend that you wear contacts as glasses can scratch and damage the headset lenses.

Our headsets have adjustable tabs under each lens so that players can adjust their vision.

WHO CAN OR CAN'T PLAY?

Players must be 13 years and over to play.

Players under the influence of alcohol or drugs will not be allowed to play. You will not receive a refund or credit.

You will need to be able to walk around, unassisted for an hour with full use of both hands. People with vision in one eye are able to play.

For safety reasons anyone with the following impairment/medical conditions are not able to play:

  • Epilepsy and similar seizure disorders.
  • Deaf or severe hearing loss.
  • Broken leg or arm.
  • Wheelchair users.

Our experiences are not recommended for anyone with the following impairment/medical conditions:

  • Heart condition.
  • Heavily pregnant.

TWILL I WALK INTO A WALL?

No. We have in-game safety mechanisms to stop that happening. It is up to you to pay attention and follow all instructions at all times to remain safe.

HOW BIG A SPACE CAN I MOVE IN?

You will have 350 square metres of game space, but the virtual space is much larger. We reuse the space with some nifty tricks we have developed.

IS THERE PARKING AVAILABLE?

Yes. We have plenty of parking in front of our venues on private property and off street.

REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY

We do not issue refunds for a change of mind or gift vouchers. Please contact us if you are not satisfied with your purchase so that we can resolve any problems.

You have up to two weeks before a booking date to cancel or re-schedule your booking. We do not accept last-minute cancellations.

Bookings can only be cancelled at the discretion of Ventus Entertainment PTY LTD. Cancelled tickets will be credited to the purchaser's Ventus VR Arena account only.

Can I buy a gift voucher?

Yes. To buy the ultimate VR experience to give as a gift, visit our E-Gift Vouchers Page.

Is there parking available?

Yes. We have plenty of parking in front of our venues on private property and off street.
